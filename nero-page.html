Red Queen

Red Queen possesses no demonic powers of her own, but she transforms into a living extension of Nero's character through her unique fighting style. Her ability to charge herself with fiery energy through the Exceed system and unleash explosive bursts with precision turns every blow into a statement of intent: impulsive, youthful, defiant, yet with heart. The Exceed system demands pinpoint timing, reflecting Nero's technical mastery. It's not just about hitting hard, but doing so with rhythm, style, and calculated aggression. Red Queen is not an heirloom or a trophy won in combat: she is a custom-molded tool, specifically tuned to his strength and fighting style. Only he can wield it properly. Therefore, more than a weapon, Red Queen is a choice. A manifestation of identity. Its spark is ignited not only by the fuel Nero injects into it, but by the character with which he wields it.
